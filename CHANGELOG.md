### Added
### ESP-IDF-MPU6050 v1.0
- Initial release of ESP-IDF MPU6050 component utilizing newest I2C libraries.
- Capabilities include:
    - Initializing the MPU6050 sensor.
    - Calibrating the accelerometer and gyroscope sensors if desired.
    - Reading accelerometer, gyroscope, and temperature sensors.
